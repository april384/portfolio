Detective

A detective investigates crimes and tries to solve them before it goes to a person with higher authority. The salary for being a detective is about 60-90k per year. You need a high school or a GED and some agencies will require a bachelor's or associate degree. Instead of getting the degree you can do an equal amount of military experience. Some skills needed to be a detective include good problem solving, critical thinking, communication, understand human behavior and psychology, and pay attention to detail.

universities

Uni #1 University of Illinois college of Law

The tuition for UIC of law is about 60-70k. Some requirements to apply include transcripts for academic performances, a valid LSAT, resume, two letters of recommendation, test of english as a foreign language if english isn't your 1st language, and a physical test. Some amenities include apartment and transportation.

Uni #2 Ohio state University

The tuition for is about 40k. Some requirements to enter are high school transcripts, ACT/SAT, and additional test scores. Some amenities include adventure recreation center, aquatic center, esports arena, Jesse Owens North and South, North recreational center, outdoor adventure center, and outdoor parks.
